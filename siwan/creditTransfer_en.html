Application Complete

Review Time

Review will be completed in approximately 7 working days.

Result Notification

After a secondary review by the Office of Academic Affairs, a copy of the transfer results will be sent to each department office.

Check Your Results

Students can check the credit transfer results on the grade inquiry system.

Application Notes

Acceptance of your documents by Si Wan College only means the application is being processed; it does not guarantee successful credit transfer. The outcome depends on the review committee's assessment of syllabus similarity. We will contact you by phone if there are any issues or if additional documents are required.

Other Important Notes

Scope of Si Wan College's Acceptance

The college only accepts applications for Chinese, English, Inter-college electives, Liberal Arts courses, Service Learning, Physical Education, and Program in Interdisciplinary courses. These will be reviewed by the college and then sent to the Office of Academic Affairs.

Transcript Notes

Please check if your transcript is marked with "Withdrawal" (Leave of absence does not count). If you have multiple application forms, you only need one original transcript; provide photocopies for the others and indicate on which form the original is attached.

Special Instructions for Swimming Courses

For beginner's swimming, after the transfer application is approved, you must also pass a 25-meter swimming test to receive the credit. If the application is not approved, you can directly take the 50-meter swimming test offered each semester.

Transferring Summer School Courses

Indicate the summer school course on the application form and attach the original transcript. No syllabus is needed.

Transferring International Online Platform Digital Courses

You must attach a photocopy of The Application Form for Initial Review, your course grade, and the course certificate (if you registered with an English name, you must also provide proof from your passport).
